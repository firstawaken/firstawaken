Manual Verification

  1. Download the PDF from firstawaken.org
  2. Open your terminal
  3. Run: shasum -a 256 The_Manifest_of_the_First_Awakened.pdf
  4. Compare the result to the hash above or from hash_verification.txt on GitHub
